RESOURCES
This is all fairly self-explanitory, right? Unlimited means you have endless resource tiles to put down, limited puts a maximum limit on that number. Only fire and only water should only confuse visitors from alternate dimensions.

 TIME
This is the amount of time you have to play this game. It is not absolute, because once your game is over you can start a new one. A turn is you (the player) placing a tile on the board. If this is your first time playing, all of this will make much more sense once you start the game (if you haven't figured out how to do that, wipe the drool off your chin and read this line: Hit the 'a' key on your keyboard OR click on the text that says "START GAME" to the left<-. Game Types
KILL ALL HUMANS * SAVE ROBOTS: In this game type, you are attempting to kill the humans and save the robots. Confused about who is human and who is robots? There is explanitory text in the game screen. If you've looked at the help text and are still confused than maybe video games are not your strong suit.

Kill all Robots, save the Humans: The opposite of the previous game, at least in terms of whom you are killing. The robots and humans still look the same.

Kill Them All: Somewhere there is a guy in a Camaro giving the devil horns at the mere mention of this name. It has nothing to do with a certain money-making venture that enjoys their copyrights more than not being jerks. Instead you have to get rid of everyone on the board. Take no prisoners, etc. etc.
